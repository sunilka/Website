A third eye for visually impaired

Watch Video
01.

Concept

A product which assists the visually impaired people and makes their life easier by voicing out day to day necessary activites by just a press of a button.

  • Personal assistant
  • Easy to use
  • Statifies most requirements
02.

Utility

Product useful for any user with visual disability. Product functions efficiently for partial visual impaired people and also for fully visually imapired people.

  • Partially visual impaired user
  • Fully visual impaired user
  • Project enthusiasts
03.

Independence

Using the smart vision system eliminates the need of blind stick or guide dogs or any other form of additional devices.The product does work independently.

  • Eliminates blind stick
  • Eliminates guide dogs
  • Provides combined features
